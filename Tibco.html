Course Details

More details

  • 01. Basics of the TIBCO BW5

    Understand the basics of the TIBCO BW5 architecture and its components, such as the TIBCO Designer, the TIBCO Administrator, and the TIBCO Runtime Agent.

  • 02. Train about the different types of process models

    We will Train about the different types of process models and understand how to create, deploy, and manage them.

  • 03. Train about the different types of activities available in TIBCO BW5

    As we embark on this informative training journey together, we will take an in-depth look at the diverse array of activities available within TIBCO BW5, exploring their unique features and functionalities in order to fully grasp how they can be effectively utilized to design, develop and implement intricate and efficient process flows that meet the specific needs of our organization.

  • 04. Train about the different types of data sources and data formats

    Throughout this training session, we will immerse ourselves in the various types of data sources and formats that are supported by TIBCO BW5, including but not limited to databases, flat files, and XML, gaining a thorough understanding of the capabilities and limitations of each and how to effectively integrate them into our process flows to achieve optimal results.

  • 05. Train about the different types of data transformations

    As we embark on this training journey, we will explore the various types of data transformations that can be performed within TIBCO BW5, including but not limited to mapping, filtering, and aggregation, gaining a comprehensive understanding of the capabilities and best practices of each, and how to effectively implement them in our process flows to achieve accurate and efficient data transformation and data manipulation.

  • 06. Train about the different types of messaging and communication

    Throughout this training program, we will delve into the various types of messaging and communication options that are available in TIBCO BW5, including but not limited to JMS, HTTP, and SOAP, gaining a comprehensive understanding of their capabilities, best practices and how to effectively implement them in our process flows to achieve seamless and efficient communication and messaging between different systems and applications.

  • 07. Train about the different types of error handling and exception

    In this training session, we will delve into the various techniques and strategies for managing errors and exceptions in TIBCO BusinessWorks 5, including error handling and exception management methods, in order to ensure that our BW5 applications are robust, reliable, and able to effectively handle unexpected situations

  • 08. Train about the different types of testing and debugging tools

    As we embark on this training journey, we will immerse ourselves in the different types of testing and debugging tools that are available within TIBCO BW5, such as the TIBCO Hawk and the TIBCO Administrator, gaining a thorough understanding of their capabilities, best practices and how to effectively utilize them to test and debug our process flows, ensuring that they are running smoothly and correctly, and identifying and resolving any issues that may arise

  • 09. Train about the different types of deployment and management

    We will be providing a comprehensive training on the various deployment and management options available in TIBCO BusinessWorks 5, including the use of the TIBCO Administrator, the TIBCO Deployment Agent, and the TIBCO Runtime Agent, to effectively deploy, manage and monitor the performance of your BW5 applications in different environments. Additionally, we will also cover best practices and tips for optimizing the performance and scalability of your BW5 applications.

  • 10. Practice building real-world projects and experimenting with different features of TIBCO BW5

    Throughout this course, we will actively engage in the practice of building real-world projects and experimenting with the diverse range of features offered by TIBCO BW5, gaining hands-on experience with the tools, techniques and best practices for creating, testing and deploying process flows. Additionally, we will also gain the skills to troubleshoot and resolve any issues that may arise, allowing us to confidently design, implement and maintain process flows that meet the specific needs of our organization.
